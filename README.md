Aura: The Biometric Music Social Network
Aura is a next-generation music platform designed to bridge the gap between human emotion and digital sound. It combines the social engagement of Instagram with advanced Biometric AI to curate a music experience that doesn't just play songs—it understands how you feel.

✨Core Features:- 
Biometric Mood Detection: Using real-time heart rate (HRV), facial micro-expressions, and vocal analysis to determine your "Aura" (Current Mood).
The Aura Feed: A vertical, video-first social feed where users share "Aura Loops"—synchronized music and visual gradients that represent their current state.
Synchronous Listening: Private and group "Pulse Rooms" where friends can listen to music in perfect sync with integrated real-time chat.
AI-Powered Curation: Our recommendation engine uses V (Valence) and A (Arousal) vectors to match your physiological data with the perfect track.
🛠️ The Tech Stack
Frontend: [e.g., React Native / Flutter] for a seamless cross-platform experience.
Backend: [e.g., Node.js / Python Fast API] to handle high-concurrency social interactions.
AI/ML: On-device emotion recognition using [e.g., TensorFlow Lite / CoreML].
Biometrics: Integration with HealthKit (iOS) and Google Fit (Android) for physiological data points.
🚀 Future Roadmap
Wearable Integration: Real-time syncing with Apple Watch and Garmin.
Aura Minting: Capability to turn unique "Mood Moments" into shareable digital collectibles.
Spatial Audio Chat: 3D voice environments for the Pulse Rooms.
🔒 Privacy First
We believe biometrics are deeply personal. Aura processes facial and physiological data on-device. We store "Mood Vectors," not raw biometric data, ensuring your privacy remains yours.
